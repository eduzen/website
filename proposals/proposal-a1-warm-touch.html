"Some people find it weird that I have a degree in philosophy. But rational thinking is something that my profession and studies share." — Eduardo, on the about page 🤓

Philosophy shaped my analytical thinking and fostered my curiosity. Nietzsche, Descartes, Foucault — they taught me to question assumptions before writing a single line of code. Writers too: Borges, Ursula K. Le Guin, Arthur C. Clarke — they taught me that good systems tell stories.

I've been in the IT industry for 15+ years, most of it as a backend developer. But I also lead teams, teach classes, and build side projects at odd hours. My goal as a teacher is not just to impart knowledge — it's to guide you in a way that you can lose me and be independent.
